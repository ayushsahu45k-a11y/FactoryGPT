## 🚀 ULTIMATE FACTORYGPT MASTER PROMPT

The living Digital Twin of FactoryGPT must represent an extremely realistic, high-fidelity, AAA-quality smart manufacturing floor. Form and function must integrate beautifully to aid workers in instant situational awareness, safety verification, and telemetry monitoring.

### 🏢 Architectural Design
- **Ceiling Heights**: 40-meter exposed architectural steel beams and roof trusses.
- **Walkways**: High-strength transparent glass paths, suspended catwalks and steel mezzanines.
- **Accessibility Elements**: Symmetrical yellow/black safety warning stripes, safety railings, custom ceiling-mounted crane systems.
- **Interconnects**: Overhead copper busways, complex heavy cable trays, and ventilation/cryogenic conduits.

### 🎨 Material Mastery & Wear & Tear
- **PBR Render Physics**: Brushed aluminum, sandblasted titanium alloy, heavy-duty insulated rubber, and industrial raw floor concrete.
- **Living Wear Effects**: Subtle fingerprints on clean glass touch panels, surface micro-scratches on reflective metals, oil drops, tire treads from autonomous vehicles, and minor corner weld rust.
- **Environmental Particles**: Ambient dust specks floating under sharp god rays, localized vent steam release, and heat haze surrounding thermo-engines.

### 💡 Global Illumination & Light Mechanics
- **Direct & Indirect Sources**: Cold-blue diagnostic LEDs (status cues) paired with high-temperature warm white overhead lighting.
- **Visual Priorities**: Avoid saturated primary colors. Soft bloom on light sources instead of dramatic full-screen glows.
- **Flickering System States**: Symmetrical blinking indicators on server clusters representing deep diagnostic workloads.

### 🛡️ Safety & Regulatory Standards
- **Compliance Benchmarks**: Full adherence to ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and OSHA safety protocols.
- **Status Accents**: Clear visual priority for emergency, warn, and nominal states. Quick diagnostic triggers.
